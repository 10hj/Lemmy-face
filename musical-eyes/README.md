# Musical Eyes

A responsive joyful singing expression based on the supplied Figma design.

## Sequence

1. Round eyes bounce gently to the beat while the pupils follow the notes.
2. Two rhythmic blinks lead into happy upper-half crescent eyes.
3. The happy crescents groove briefly before the round eyes reopen.
4. Three exact Figma note shapes move on independent rhythms throughout the loop.

No dependencies or build step are required. The animation pauses while the browser tab is hidden and respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.
