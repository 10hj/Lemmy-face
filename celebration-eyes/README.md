# Celebration Eyes

A joyful celebration expression with rhythmic eyes, happy crescents, fireworks, and confetti.

## Sequence

1. Excited round eyes bounce and look around.
2. A quick double blink transitions into happy upper-half crescent eyes.
3. Fireworks repeatedly burst outward while colorful confetti drifts across the frame.
4. The celebration fades and the round eyes reopen.

No dependencies or build step are required. The animation pauses while the browser tab is hidden and respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.
